Our color system relies on semantic tokens, simplifying design decisions and enabling dark theme support.

## Background Colors

Background colors are accessed through the `backgroundColor` prop in the `lx` style object.

<ColorTable category="bg" />

## Text Colors

Text colors are accessed through the `color` prop in the `lx` style object.

<ColorTable category="text" />

## Border Colors

Border colors are accessed through the `borderColor` prop in the `lx` style object.

<ColorTable category="border" />

## Crypto Colors

Cryptocurrency brand colors are accessed through `theme.colors.crypto.*`. These colors are not available directly in the `lx` prop but can be used in custom styles.

<ColorTable category="crypto" />

## Discovery Colors

Discovery service and partner brand colors are accessed through `theme.colors.discover.*`. These colors are not available directly in the `lx` prop but can be used in custom styles.
